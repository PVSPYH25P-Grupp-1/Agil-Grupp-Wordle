How to play

Quess the Wordle in 6 tries. Each guess must be a valid 5-letter word. The color of the tiles will change to show how close your guess was to the word. If a letter is in the word but in the wrong position, it is marked with a yellow background. If a letter is in the word and in the correct position, it is marked with a green background. If a letter is not in the word, it is marked with a gray background.
